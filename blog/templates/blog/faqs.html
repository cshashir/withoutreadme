-- General Queations --

What does Partshala do?

Partshala is a non-profit initiative which provides temporary staffing solutions to small, medium and large businesses. We advertise, authenticate, interview and provide suitable staff as per the job requirement.

Who can post the job vacancies?

Any small, mid or large business which requires young man-power to meet its operations can register and post job vacancies on Partshala to become our Associate. The list of job categories that can be advertised is mentioned on the Home page. Partshala offers flexibility to an associate to post job vacancy for as short as 2 days.

Who can apply for jobs?

Any person who is seeking for a part-time job can apply for any suitable posted job and become our Fellow. A fellow need not be highly qualified in terms of education or skill (minimum 10th passed). The fellow can be a student, competitive exam aspirant or even a part-timer who is already employed and seeks extra income. The fellow needs to meet the job requirements to be qualified for the job, thatâ€™s it. The fellow needs to be above 16 years of age to comply with child labour laws.

How does the fellow gets paid? Who pays the fellow?

Payment is directly processed by the associate towards the fellow as per the job advertisement. Partial payment can be made out on daily/ half period/ weekly/ monthly basis or fully at the end of the job as stated by the employer in the advertisement.

How does Partshala pay the fellow?

Partshala does not deal with or monitor any transaction. Employers (Associates) are obliged to pay directly to the fellow in accordance with the advertisement. Any deviation from the advertised salary needs to be justified to Partshala before carrying out any payment related transaction with the fellow. Payment related grievances can be raised by the fellow or the associate and Partshala would take appropriate actions.

-- Employers/ Associates --

What jobs can be posted here?

Any part-time or full time job that needs hard working and dedicated youth power can be advertised on the portal. Since Partshala is committed to cater young and learning fellows, it is a request that the advertisers/ jobs try not to demand for high educational qualification such as graduation degree or prior work experience.

What happens after the associate posts a job?

Employer would be contacted regarding their job posts for better understanding the nature of the job and authentication purpose. Only those applications which match with the job requirement would be forwarded to the employer.

Why is my job post not visible on the portal?

Once the job is posted by the associate, they would be contacted by Partshala for better understanding of the nature of the job and authentication purposes. Jobs will be visible on the portal only after Partshala verifies it.

Can an associate post more than one job?

Yes. The associate can post any number of jobs or vacancy without any limit. It is advised to use this portal and make best of it for their business

-- Employees/ Fellows --

What happens when a fellow applies for a specific job?

The fellow would be contacted for the interview and authentication purposes. Only authenticated fellow can apply for jobs. Every fellow is interviewed and if matched with the job requirements, their application is forwarded to the associate for final approval.

Can a registered fellow apply for any job?

Yes. A fellow can apply for any job once he gets authenticated. Once the fellow registers with Partshala, they are contacted for identity verification. It is a small but important process to build trust in the whole ecosystem. Only a verified fellow can apply for jobs.

Why does my application has status as 'under verification'?

Once a job is applied by an authenticated fellow, they would be interviewed by Partshala on phone call. Only those applicants/ fellows who meet the job requirements would be notified to the associate.

Why does my application has status as 'Rejected'?

If your application status shows 'Rejected', it means that you donâ€™t meet the job requirements. Please apply for another job.

Can a fellow apply for more than one job?

Yes. The fellow can apply for multiple jobs as long as the job dates and timings do not interfere with another job. It is requested not to apply for clashing jobs as such applications would be rejected by Partshala during the verification process.

-- Registering Complaint or Feedback --

How can associates or fellows register their complaint?

Any associate or fellow can contact us through the respective job application. Contact us for any grievance. We would take appropriate action. Partshala commits to provide complete customer satisfaction.

How to report the absence of a fellow?

If a fellow takes any uninformed leave, associate can register this grievance in the fellow's application. Fellow would be penalised with the day's payment and a strict warning. S/he would not be paid for absent days. Any subsequent misconduct would result in black-listing of the fellow by Partshala.

How to report misconduct of the fellow?

If a fellow acts inappropriate, associate can register this grievance in the fellow's application. Partshala commits to take strict action. Based on the gravity of the misconduct fellow can be blacklisted by Partshala.

How does the rating system help an associate or a fellow?

Partshala provides an opportunity to review and rate the entire ecosystem. An associate can rate a fellow based on his/her service and adherence to the job requirement. While the fellow can rate the associate based on the work culture, hygeine, etc. This past record helps in building a preliminary trust among the associate and fellow. An associate can chose to accept or reject a fellow's application based on the qualification and past performance. Likewise, a fellow can chose to apply for a particular job based on the associate's past rating.

How does the rating system help Partshala?

Past records of an associate or fellow help Partshala in taking appropriate actions in order to maintain the standard of its services. Partshala would use this rating during the verification of the job posts and applications. It can even blacklist an associate or fellow on account of misconducts, if any.
